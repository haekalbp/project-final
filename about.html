Thunder Gym

is an international fitness centre brand founded in 2013 in the Jakarta, Indonesia. The company owned and operated its clubs around the world, until financial pressures saw parts of the company sold off to various owners in different regions between 2016 and 2017. The Thunder Gym brand has been retained by its new owners in most cases.

Since our humble beginnings in 2013, we have become one of the largest fitness brands in the world, and the largest in Southeast Asia. A trusted and enduring brand, we have an unrivalled network in the region, with knowledgeable teams, new technology and world class clubs. Through the years, we’ve seen a lot of changes and we’ve come a long way. But our purpose remains clear: we are the fitness leaders who inspire people to go further in life.

With more than 230,000 members around the region, we’re always looking for new ways to support and inspire them – and not just in the gym.
